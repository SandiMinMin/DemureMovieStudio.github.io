Four types of Coffee Beans around the world

Arabica Bean

Sourced from the lush highlands of Ethiopia, our 100% Arabica beans deliver a smooth, rich flavor with notes of berry and floral undertones. Low in bitterness and freshly roasted, these beans promise a true taste of Ethiopian coffee heritage in every cup.

Robusta Bean

From the tropical regions of Vietnam, our 100% Robusta beans bring a bold, full-bodied flavor with earthy, nutty notes and a touch of natural bitterness. High in caffeine and freshly roasted, these beans are perfect for a strong, energizing cup that packs a punch.

Liberica Bean

Harvested from the lush regions of the Philippines, our 100% Liberica beans offer an exotic, smoky flavor with hints of floral and woody undertones. Known for their unique, larger beans, Liberica delivers a bold, aromatic cup that is perfect for adventurous coffee lovers seeking something extraordinary.

Excelsa Bean

Sourced from the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, our 100% Excelsa beans offer a distinctive, fruity flavor with tart, wine-like notes and a complex aroma. Known for their depth and versatility, Excelsa beans bring a bold twist to your coffee experience, perfect for those who enjoy a layered, refreshing cup.
