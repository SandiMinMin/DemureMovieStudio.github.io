Coffee Brewer

French Press

The French Press brews rich, full-bodied coffee by steeping coarsely ground beans in hot water, using a plunger with a fine mesh filter.

Grind Size: Coarse.
Ratio: 1:15 coffee to water.
Temp & Time: 200°F (93°C), steep for 4 minutes.
Cleanup: Rinse the filter and pot after use.

Pour-Over

Pour-over brewing allows for precise control over extraction, highlighting unique flavors pouring of hot water over coffee grounds.

Grind Size: Medium.
Ratio: 1:16 coffee to water.
Temp & Time: 200°F (93°C), brew for 3-4 minutes.
Technique: Pour in a circular motion for even saturation.

$50

Aeropress

The Aeropress uses air pressure to brew smooth coffee quickly, making it ideal for espresso-like coffee and cold brews.

Grind Size: Fine to medium.
Ratio: 1:15 coffee to water.
Temp & Time: 200°F (93°C), brew for 30-60 seconds.
Cleanup: Clean immediately after use.

$60

Drip Coffee Maker

A convenient machine that brews multiple cups of coffee automatically by dripping hot water through coffee grounds.

Grind Size: Medium.
Ratio: 1:17 coffee to water.
Water Quality: Use filtered water.
Maintenance: Clean regularly and replace filters.

$40

Cold Brew Maker

Cold brew makers steep coffee grounds in cold water for 12-24 hours, resulting in a smooth, less acidic coffee concentrate.

Grind Size: Coarse.
Ratio: 1:4 coffee to water.
Steeping: 12-24 hours in the fridge.
Storage: Keep concentrate in the fridge for up to two weeks.

$200

Moka Pot

The Moka pot brews strong, espresso-like coffee using steam pressure from boiling water, making it a favorite for rich coffee lovers.

Grind Size: Fine to medium.
Filling: Level the coffee grounds without packing.
Heat: Medium heat until bubbling starts.
Serving: Remove from heat to prevent bitterness.
